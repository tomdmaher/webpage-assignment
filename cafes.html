TOP LONDON CAFÉS

It's here, don't miss out on Tom Maher's shortlist of cafés you must visit in London!

Hard Rock Café

What's special?

Listen to the live music at Hard Rock Café whilst enjoying their amazing American food.

How to get there

Location: 150 Old Park Lane, London, W1K 1QZ

Nearest station: Hyde Park Corner Underground Station

Nearest bus routes: 5, 9, 22, N9, N22, N97

Caffé Concerto

What's special?

Want some cake? Come to Caffé Concerto for the best cake in London.

How to get there

Location: 79-81 Regent St, Mayfair, London, W1B 4EG

Nearest station: Piccadilly Circus Underground Station

Nearest bus routes: 139, N15, N18, N113

The Breakfast Club

What's special?

Feeling hungry in the morning? Visit The Breakfast Club for a big plate of tasty pancakes.

How to get there

Location: 33 D'Arblay St, Soho, London, W1F 8EU

Nearest station: Tottenham Court Road Underground Station

Nearest bus routes: 55, 98, N25, N55, N98, N207

Patisserie Valerie

What's special?

Relax whilst having afternoon tea at Patisserie Valerie, beautiful sandwiches and cakes.

How to get there

Location: 116A High St, Sutton, SM1 1LU

Nearest station: Sutton Station

Nearest bus routes: 80, 151, 164, 213, 280, 407, 413, 420, 470, 613, 820, S1, S3, S4
